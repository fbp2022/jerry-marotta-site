About Jerry

GOLD SEAL
FLIGHT INSTRUCTOR

Finding a reliable flight instructor who truly focuses on your individual needs can be challenging. As a FAA Gold Seal Certified Flight Instructor, Jerry Marotta brings an approach that motivates and guides students toward real-world competency and confidence in the cockpit.

Jerry designs every lesson around his students' specific goals — from first-time discovery flights to advanced maneuver training and checkride preparation. His instruction style emphasizes positive, constructive techniques that build lasting skills and genuine confidence.

Based in Knoxville, Tennessee, Jerry serves student pilots of all skill levels across the greater East Tennessee region.
